FAQ

Where does the data come from? ▾
PayScope combines public job advertisements (to reflect what employers are offering today) with trusted public datasets, including official earnings benchmarks, to add context and stability.
How often is it updated? ▾
Live market data is refreshed on a scheduled basis. Official datasets update on their published cadence. The platform is designed to surface both “right now” movement and longer-term baselines.
How accurate is advertised pay? ▾
Advertised pay reflects what employers choose to publish. When ranges or missing values exist, PayScope uses sensible rules and normalisation so you still get useful comparisons and trends.
Is this compliant / GDPR-friendly? ▾
PayScope is built around public job ads and official datasets, not personal profiles. This landing page does not store submitted emails — waitlist/access uses your email client.
Can I use this for any sector? ▾
Yes — PayScope is designed for all sectors, with role/sector normalisation so comparisons remain practical. Coverage varies by market data availability, and the platform is built to make those gaps visible.
